# Review

## Scope

Read-only review of the local persona seed and the visible Naver Cafe discussion-board lists.

## Findings

- The local seed has a sound safety boundary and 20 distinguishable starting personas.
- It needs a production layer: title queue, variation tracker, drafts, and a no-repeat ledger.
- Live board evidence favors concrete beginner, event, and local-context situations over empty small talk.
- Unverified details (venues, schedules, products, health guidance) must not be fabricated in generated drafts.

## Regression/Security

- No original content was altered.
- No post, comment, login credential, or external setting was changed.

## Recommended Next Step

Generate a small controlled batch of locally saved drafts from the approved patterns, then review wording and repetition before any publishing decision.
